CINNAMOROLL
CLOUD LAND

Welcome to Our Village!

The village consists of a town of houses and a city-like
village. In the main town circle, you can find the Supermarket,
the Hospital, and the Cloud Nursery! Other areas beyond the
main circle include:

  • Shopping Street
  • Cozy Cafe
  • The Neighborhood
  • Amusement Park
  • Shopping Mall







~ Shopping Street ~

Shopping Street is the go-to place for hangouts with
friends like Milk, Mocha, and Chiffon! It has the Music
Store, the Ice Cream Shop, the Book Store, and the Flower
Shop. You can take a ton of cute photos and buy lots of
interactable supplies here, too! Sometimes one of
Cinnamoroll's friends will ask you to buy them something
from here as part of a quest.













~ Cozy Cafe! ~

Cinnamoroll's Cozy Cafe is a place where you can earn
money baking delicious desserts and unique drinks.
You can work as both a chef or a waiter!
It's fun talking to the other Sanrio characters, too.

















~ Home Sweet Home ~

Here's your own personalized home where you can raise
your Cloud Spirits by giving them food, putting them
to bed, and playing games with them! You can also
customize your home by buying furniture and editing
your layout. Oftentimes, each floor is purchased with
its own pre-decorated furniture.













In your cozy little home, you also have your own garden!
You can plant carrots, sunflowers, pumpkins, cherries,
and anything else that's available in the Seed Shop. If
you're lucky enough, sometimes you'll be able to grow a
piece of plant-themed furniture instead of a plant.
